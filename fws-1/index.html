The Free Software Wing (FSW) at GRIET (Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology) promotes the use and development of open-source software among students. It focuses on providing a platform for students to explore, contribute, and collaborate on free software projects. The FSW conducts workshops, seminars, and coding sessions, encouraging students to embrace a culture of knowledge sharing and innovation. By fostering a strong community around open-source initiatives, the Free Software Wing helps students develop technical skills, contribute to real-world projects, and better understand the ethical and practical implications of using free software.
